About Me

A veteran professional with nearly two decades of experience in sales and marketing, married with four children, I take pride in maintaining a strong and stable family life alongside my professional commitments, reflecting a balanced and disciplined approach to success.

Over the years, my career and personal endeavors have offered me extensive international exposure. In 2009, I traveled to Thailand on an office-sponsored retreat, expanding my understanding of team dynamics in multicultural settings. That same year, I led an office project in Dubai, gaining firsthand experience of the region’s fast-paced commercial environment. In 2012, I visited Saudi Arabia for a deeply meaningful pilgrimage, enriching my cultural and spiritual perspective.

Today, I hold a five-year multiple-entry U.S. visitor visa and a ten-year Canadian visitor visa, reflecting my readiness for global engagement and my ability to participate in cross-border opportunities with ease. As a responsible citizen, I also maintain all essential legal credentials, including national ID, passport, driving license, and taxpayer registration. Collectively, these experiences and qualifications position me as a well-rounded professional who values integrity, family, and global awareness.

Professional Accumen

As a leader in corporate client management, I specialize in building and executing strategies that align business objectives with customer needs. My role spans the complete commercial ecosystem, from identifying the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) and refining buyer personas, to mapping the buyer journey and addressing client pain points with tailored solutions.

I oversee the end-to-end sales process and marketing funnel, ensuring every stage i.e. awareness, consideration, and decision, delivers measurable impact. By designing and implementing a robust sales enablement strategy, I empower teams with the right tools, insights, and playbooks to drive consistent performance. This enables me to create added value through cross-selling and upselling opportunities, while maintaining a strong focus on customer care and retention.

My leadership philosophy is centered on long-term relationship building. For nearly two decades, I have cultivated strong, trust-based partnerships with corporate clients, ensuring their growth goals are met while driving sustainable business results for my organization. This commitment to excellence, combining strategic sales enablement, customer-centric execution, and relationship management has allowed me to transform one-time engagements into lasting collaborations.
